Termux update without losing data:
- Your data is in data/db.json.
- Before replacing files, backup it:
  cp -r data data-backup
- Extract new zip into a NEW folder, then copy old data folder into it:
  cp -r /old/path/data /new/path/
- Or replace all files except data/.

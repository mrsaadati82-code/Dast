Dast Rast Phase 1

Run without npm install:
1) Extract zip
2) Double click RUN-WINDOWS-NO-INSTALL.bat
3) Open http://localhost:8787

Default admin:
admin@dastrast.local
Admin12345

Implemented in this phase:
- Fixed bottom navigation: sticky inside app viewport; pages scroll above it.
- Bottom menu: Home, Assistant, Reports, Cheques, Persons.
- Hamburger menu: Persons, Accounts, Customer Receivables, Expert Settlements, History, Income/Expense, Categories, Ledger, Assistant Training, Treasury, Admin.
- Financial dashboard KPI cards.
- Customer receivables with quick search.
- Persons debtor/creditor ledger.
- Expert settlement module: experts, manual payment, settlement history, balance.
- Income/Expense module.
- Treasury module: cash/bank accounts, deposit, withdraw, ledger.
- Local assistant commands for transactions, cheques, persons, settlement, projects, experts, treasury.
